Fairmount Bicycles is a full service bike shop located at 2015 Fairmount Avenue. The shop carries a selection of new and refurbished bicycles for commuting, touring, and entry-level road racing. You will also find accessories for every day and recreational use, from helmets, locks, and lights to racks, bags, and fenders. Bicycle rentals are available year-round. Our service department is capable of tuning any machine, from vintage to modern. Tune-ups are only $55, and smaller quick fixes are possible at reasonable a la carte prices.

In season, regular store hours are Tuesday - Sunday 10am-7pm. Conveniently located near the Eastern State Penitentiary, museums, and other businesses along Fairmount Ave, the shop is a great place to rent a bicycle or browse a selection of new and refurbished bicycles. Fairmount Bicycles welcomes you to stop in and say hello. Stop on by to find the right bicycle to make your life better or just to visit a place where bicycles are loved.
